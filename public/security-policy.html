> Responsible Disclosure Policy

At debasisbiswas.me, security is a priority. If you discover a vulnerability, we encourage responsible disclosure to help us resolve it quickly and safely.

> How to Report

Email your findings to: forensic@debasisbiswas.me

> What to Include

  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce (Proof of Concept)
  • Optional contact details for follow-up

> Rules of Engagement

  • No public disclosure before resolution
  • Do not access, modify, or delete any data
  • Do not use automated tools or cause service disruption

> Our Commitment

  • Timely acknowledgment of your report
  • Clear communication during investigation
  • Proper resolution of validated issues

Thank you for helping make the web safer.
